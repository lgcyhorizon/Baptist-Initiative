Governance Structure

How the Initiative is organized.

The Board of Directors oversees the Initiative’s ministry. The Advisory Board offers counsel without voting privileges; all Advisory Board members serve as Regional Mentors. An Administrator handles day-to-day operations. Approved Training Centers and Regional Mentors deliver our uniform certification curriculum across the country.

Membership

Stand with the Initiative.

Two paths into membership — one for those who want to support and learn alongside us, and one for certified biblical counselors who carry the work into the local church.

Home · Membership
Two Tracks

Choose how to walk with us.

Both tracks affirm our Statement of Faith and our Affirmations & Denials. The difference is the level of training and commitment.

Track 1

Associate Membership

For believers who affirm our convictions and want to grow in counseling competency — pastors’ wives, small-group leaders, lay counselors, and church members of every stripe.

  • Affirm our Statement of Faith & Affirmations and Denials
  • Access to all written, audio, and video resources
  • Discounted registration for conferences and symposiums
  • Invitation to regional gatherings
  • Quarterly member newsletter
Apply for Associate
Track 2

Certified Membership

For those who have completed the three-phase certification process and serve as biblical counselors in a local church or ministry context.

  • Completed Phase One, Two, and Three of certification
  • Passed the Process and Theology exams
  • Completed 50 supervised counseling hours
  • Annual recertification & continuing education
  • Listed in our public Find-a-Counselor directory
  • Voting privileges in member matters
Begin Certification
Why Become a Member?

You are not meant to grow alone.

1

Stay sharpened

Continuing education and access to fresh resources keep your counseling rooted in the Word.

2

Be sent

Plug into a network of regional mentors, training centers, and church-based counseling ministries.

3

Stand together

Speak with one voice about the sufficiency of Scripture — with brothers and sisters who hold the line with you.

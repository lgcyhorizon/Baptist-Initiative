Sister Initiatives

The Initiative family.

The Baptist Initiative shares convictions and resources with several related ministries.

Baptist Association of Biblical Counselors

A fellowship of Baptist churches and pastors committed to biblical counseling as the ordinary work of the local church.

Baptist Association of Biblical Evangelists

An equipping network for Baptist evangelists who hold the inseparability of evangelism and discipleship.

Baptist Initiative for Biblical Counseling

Our parent initiative for “every member a counselor” training in the local Baptist church.

Biblical Counseling International

Cross-cultural training and certification of biblical counselors around the world.

Biblical Discipleship International

Discipleship and church-planting partner extending the Word into hard-to-reach contexts.

National Biblical Counseling Association

A national fellowship of biblical counselors representing many denominations.
